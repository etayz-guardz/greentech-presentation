## Requirements

1. **Structure, eco-system and main players in the selected market**  
   Consider how the value chain works, who the different players are, and the relations between them.

2. **Solution iteration**  
   Articulate your offering considering the learnings of the market.

3. **Competitive analysis review**  
   Include the different players in the field offering similar or substitute solutions, and analyze their strengths and weaknesses compared to the offered solution.

4. **Market segment iteration**  
   Articulate the specific market segment considering the competitive landscape. Position your offering vis-a-vis comparable players in the market, including both competitors and non-competitors.

5. **Business model**  
   Position your venture in the value chain and determine the business model. Categorize the business model type and refine it. Combination or novel business models are acceptable as long as they are validated and well-reasoned.
