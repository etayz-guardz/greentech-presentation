04 / 07 — Competitive LandscapeEvery existing tool is optimised for one column. The composting layer is where they structurally cannot compete.
Strengths & Weaknesses

Where each competitor is strong — and why they still can't replace us.

Category Their Strengths Their Weaknesses vs Us Threat level
Hyperscalers
AWS · GCP · Azure		 Infinite scale, existing customer trust, native lifecycle policies, partial carbon dashboards. Incentivised to retain — not delete. No cross-cloud view. No legal/compliance scoring. No ESG audit output. Medium
Data Platforms
Snowflake · DBX		 Deep structured-data access, strong ML/AI ecosystem, large enterprise install base. Structured data only — miss unstructured dark data (80% of the problem). No deletion, no ESG, no legal scoring. Low
Backup & Protection
Rubrik · Veeam		 Trusted for data safety, strong compliance workflows, broad enterprise penetration. Mission is to retain and protect — deletion is antithetical. No carbon accounting, no business-value scoring. Low
Catalog & Governance
Collibra · Atlan		 Best-in-class discovery and classification, strong DPO relationships, rich metadata graphs. Catalog without action — owners see the data but lack a deletion workflow or ESG output. No carbon axis. Medium
FinOps
Flexera · Apptio		 Deep CFO/CIO relationships, proven cost-reduction ROI, cloud-agnostic cost view. Cost lens only — no carbon, legal, or ESG framing. Cannot produce CSRD-compliant output. Medium
ESG / Sustainability
Watershed · Persefoni		 Strong CSO relationships, CSRD/SEC reporting workflows, carbon methodology credibility. Report-only — no data-layer access. Cannot reduce Scope-3 emissions, only measure them. Miss the cost and legal axes. Low
05 / 07 — S/W AnalysisMedium-threat players (hyperscalers, catalog, FinOps) could add one feature — but structural incentives prevent them from owning the full decisioning layer.
Market Segment & Positioning

Entry wedge: EU-regulated Finance & Telco — the segment where all three pressures converge.

Refined ICP
EU Finance & Telco enterprises
>$1B revenue · 1–10 PB cloud storage · active CSRD reporting obligation · DPO on staff
Why this segment first
Highest convergence of all three pressures
CSRD Scope-3 disclosure (mandatory 2025) + GDPR/DORA retention fines + FinOps cost mandate from CFO. No other segment has all three live simultaneously.
Expansion path
EU Health → UK Finance → US regulated
Each step adds one regulatory driver. US SEC climate rule activates the US market in Year 3.
